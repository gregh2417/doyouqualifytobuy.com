FAQ

Common Questions

Is this a real pre-approval from a lender? ⌄
This is an AI-powered pre-qualification report based on your documents and current lending guidelines. It is not a formal loan commitment, but it gives you — and agents or sellers — a realistic, document-backed picture of what you qualify for. A Go Direct Lenders loan officer reviews results and can issue a formal pre-approval letter. There is no cost to get your pre-qualification report.
What documents do I need to upload? ⌄
It depends on your loan type. For W-2 employees: last 2 years of W-2s, 30 days of pay stubs, and 2 years of tax returns. For self-employed: 12–24 months of bank statements and/or a P&L. For DSCR/investment: a lease agreement and bank statements. The platform tells you exactly what to upload based on the program you select.
Does this affect my credit score? ⌄
No. We do not pull your credit at any point during the pre-qualification process. There is no hard or soft inquiry. Your score is completely unaffected.
Is my data secure? ⌄
Your documents are encrypted in transit and at rest. Files are processed solely for the purpose of generating your report and are not sold or shared with third parties. Documents are deleted after your report is generated.
I'm self-employed. Can I still use this? ⌄
Yes — and this is where we shine. We support Bank Statement loans (12–24 months of statements), Non-QM programs, 1099/gig income, and DSCR for investment properties. Our AI calculates qualifying income using the same alternative methods that underwriters use.
